✦ Who We Are

About
Joy to Share

We are a youth-led social organisation founded by students of IPS SMA Xin Zhong Surabaya, united by the belief that joy multiplies when shared.

Our work focuses on breaking educational inequality by teaching Chinese and English to children from an early age, while also supporting panti asuhan and underprivileged communities with food aid and donations.

✦ What We Do

Our Programs

Four pillars of impact — education, relief, community giving, and sustainable fundraising.

📚
Language Education

We teach Chinese and English to children from an early age, building a foundation for global opportunities and broader horizons. Classes are designed to be fun, interactive, and accessible.

🇨🇳 Chinese · 🇬🇧 English
🤲
Community Relief

We distribute food and essential goods to panti asuhan and underserved communities across Surabaya — because no one should be left behind.

🏠 Panti Asuhan · 🍱 Food Aid
💚
Open Giving Platform

We create accessible opportunities for the public to donate and be part of meaningful change. Everyone can be an agent of impact — big or small.

🌐 Open to All Donors
🛍️
Products for Purpose

We sell curated products as a sustainable funding stream, turning everyday purchases into contributions toward education and charity. Shop with heart.

🛒 Shop to Give Back
